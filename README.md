# 📦 Order Fulfillment Analysis

> An end-to-end data analytics project that analyzes a real-world e-commerce supply chain dataset using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to evaluate order fulfillment performance, delivery efficiency, sales trends, and profitability across global markets.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **DataCo Supply Chain Dataset (180,519 records | 53 features)** covering order data from **January 2015 to September 2017** across five global markets: **Pacific Asia, USCA, Africa, Europe, and LATAM**. The analysis focuses on identifying operational bottlenecks and generating actionable insights to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Late deliveries and inefficient fulfillment processes directly impact customer satisfaction and profitability. With over **54.8% of orders** experiencing late delivery or cancellation, there is a clear need to identify operational inefficiencies and provide actionable recommendations.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Second Class shipping has the highest average delay (**1.99 days**)—nearly double that of First Class (**1.00 day**).
- Standard Class records **0.00 average delay**, making it the most operationally reliable shipping mode.
- Only **17.83%** of orders were delivered on time, while **54.8%** experienced late delivery.
- **7,754** orders were cancelled, representing potential revenue loss and reduced customer satisfaction.
- Central Africa (**0.64**) and South Asia (**0.60**) recorded the highest average delivery delays.
- The **Fishing** category generated the highest sales (**$6.9M**) and profit (**$0.76M**) among all 50 product categories.
- The **Computers** category recorded the lowest sales (**$0.7M**) and profit (**$0.07M**).
- March experienced the highest average delivery delay, indicating seasonal operational challenges.
- The overall profit margin is approximately **10.8%** (**$3.97M profit on $36.78M sales**), highlighting opportunities for operational optimization.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve Second Class shipping operations to reduce the **1.99-day** average delivery delay by reviewing logistics processes and service-level agreements.
- Prioritize logistics improvements in Central Africa and South Asia to minimize regional delivery delays.
- Increase the on-time delivery rate by investigating operational causes behind the current **17.83%** performance.
- Analyze the root causes of **7,754** cancelled orders and implement proactive customer communication strategies.
- Continue investing in the **Fishing** category due to its strong contribution to both sales and profitability.
- Reassess the **Computers** category to improve performance through pricing, marketing, or product strategy.
- Prepare for seasonal delivery challenges in **March** through better inventory planning and logistics readiness.
- Optimize shipping costs and fulfillment operations to improve the overall **10.8%** profit margin.
